Top Restaurants In Chennai

Bay 6 is a popular restaurant located in Akkarai, Chennai, renowned for its vibrant ambiance and diverse menu that includes seafood, North Indian, Italian, and Asian cuisines. With a picturesque beach view and rooftop seating, it offers a romantic dining experience ideal for families and special occasions. Guests frequently recommend dishes like Honey Mustard Chicken, BBQ Pizza, and Gai Prik Yang Sathy, complemented by a selection of cocktails and desserts. The average cost for two is around ₹1,800, making it an affordable choice. Bay 6 also provides amenities such as home delivery, takeaway, and valet parking, making it a trendy spot for dining and socializing.

Sea Tales Restaurant is a highly-rated dining establishment located at 5/107, Jagajeevanram Avenue, Injambakkam, Chennai, with a commendable rating of 4.7 based on over 3,600 reviews. Known for its vibrant ambiance and beach view, the restaurant specializes in seafood and offers a diverse menu that includes various Asian and continental dishes. Popular items include BBQ chicken winglets, Gai Prik Yang Sathy, and Honey Mustard Chicken. Sea Tales is open daily from 1 PM to 11 PM, with extended hours on weekends, making it a great spot for both lunch and dinner. The restaurant also provides amenities such as outdoor seating, valet parking, and a full bar, catering to families and groups looking for a relaxed dining experience.

Kipling Cafe is an elegant Asian-continental fusion restaurant situated at 16, L Jey Avenue, Spring Gardens 2nd St, Akkarai, Chennai. With a rating of 4.1 based on over 8,800 reviews, it is well-regarded for its romantic ambiance and beautiful garden seating, complemented by a sea-view roof terrace. The restaurant offers a diverse menu featuring popular dishes such as Spinach Ravioli, New York Cheesecake, Pad Thai Noodles, Risotto, and Chicken Kebab. The average cost for two people is approximately ₹1,600, making it a mid-range dining option. Kipling Cafe operates daily from 12 PM to 11 PM and provides amenities such as home delivery, takeaway, a full bar, and outdoor seating, making it a favored spot for families and couples looking for a delightful dining experience.
